# 🚀 Request Early Access

UTO is in active development and currently available **by invitation only**.

We are onboarding a limited number of engineering teams who want to replace fragile, selector-based tests with vision-first automation that runs on both web and mobile from a single framework.

---

## Who We're Looking For

- QA and engineering teams frustrated by Cypress's web-only limits or Playwright's selector fragility.
- Mobile-first teams tired of maintaining two separate test stacks (Cypress + Appium).
- Platform teams evaluating next-generation automation infrastructure for CI/CD pipelines.
- Engineering leaders looking for a defensible, low-maintenance automation layer.

---

## What Early Access Includes

- Full access to the UTO CLI (`init`, `run`, `report`, `ui`) for web and mobile.
- Priority setup support and direct access to the core team.
- Influence over the Phase 6 (UTO Studio) and Phase 7 (self-healing) roadmap.
- Early access to the visual test authoring interface before public release.

---

## How to Apply

Send a brief note to **[contact@uto.dev](mailto:contact@uto.dev)** with:

1. Your team size and current automation stack (Cypress? Playwright? Selenium?).
2. Your primary pain point (web-only? selector brittleness? mobile parity?).
3. The platform(s) you need to target (Chrome, Android, iOS, or all three).

We review applications on a rolling basis and respond within a few business days.

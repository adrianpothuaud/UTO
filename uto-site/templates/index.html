What UTO Is and Why It Matters

What Is UTO?

UTO is a unified automation platform for web and mobile execution with an operator-focused workflow. It is designed to feel complete out of the box, not stitched together from multiple tools.

Why UTO?

Most teams are trapped between brittle selectors, fragmented toolchains, and weak run transparency. UTO targets that gap with one consistent model from authoring to diagnostics.

Killer Flags

  • Single platform story across web and mobile.
  • Execution and observability as one product surface.
  • Technical depth with a roadmap designed for category leadership.
